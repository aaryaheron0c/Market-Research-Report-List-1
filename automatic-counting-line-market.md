<p><ul><li>Single Channel</li><li>Multi Channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic counting lines can be categorized into single channel and multi-channel markets based on their operational structure. In a single channel market, products are scanned and counted through one pathway, making it simpler and more cost-effective, ideal for smaller operations. Conversely, multi-channel markets utilize multiple pathways for counting, enhancing efficiency and throughput for larger-scale operations. This setup allows for simultaneous processing of various items, catering to higher demand and complex inventory needs, thus optimizing overall productivity.</p></p>
