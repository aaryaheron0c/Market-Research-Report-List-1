<p><p>The aviation turbine engine market primarily consists of turbojet and turbofan engines. Turbojet engines, characterized by their simplicity and high-speed performance, are mainly used in military aircraft and supersonic jets. In contrast, turbofan engines are more efficient for commercial airliners due to their quieter operation and better fuel economy at subsonic speeds. Turbofan engines are further categorized into low-bypass and high-bypass variants, catering to different operational needs, which enhances their dominance in commercial aviation.</p></p>
