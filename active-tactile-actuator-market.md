<p><strong>The Active Tactile Actuator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Eccentric Rotating Mass (ERM) Actuators</li><li>Linear Resonant Actuators (LRA)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Active Tactile Actuator market includes various types, primarily focusing on Eccentric Rotating Mass (ERM) actuators and Linear Resonant Actuators (LRA). ERM actuators generate vibrations by rotating an off-center mass, providing tactile feedback commonly found in mobile devices. In contrast, LRA actuators use resonant frequencies to create precise vibrations, enhancing user experience in applications like wearables and gaming. Additionally, the "Others" category encompasses alternative technologies such as piezoelectric and electrostatic actuators, catering to specialized tactile applications.</p></p>
