<p><strong>The UV-Vis Spectrometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Beam</li><li>Double-Beam</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV-Vis spectrometer market is primarily divided into single-beam and double-beam types. Single-beam spectrometers measure the intensity of light before and after it passes through a sample in a single pathway, offering simplicity and affordability. In contrast, double-beam spectrometers split the light source into two paths, one for the sample and one for a reference, allowing for greater accuracy and stability in measurements by compensating for fluctuations in light intensity. Both types serve various analytical applications across industries.</p></p>
