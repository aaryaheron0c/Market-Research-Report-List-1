<p><p>Self-priming twin screw pumps are categorized into two main types: double-suction and single-suction twin-screw pumps. Double-suction twin-screw pumps efficiently handle higher flow rates by featuring two impellers, allowing for balanced pressure and reduced vibrations. In contrast, single-suction twin-screw pumps are designed for less demanding applications, drawing fluid from one side only, making them simpler and more compact. Both types are widely used in various industries for transporting viscous fluids, offering reliability and excellent performance.</p></p>
