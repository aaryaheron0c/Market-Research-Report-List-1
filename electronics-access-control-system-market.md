<p><p>The Electronics Access Control System market is primarily categorized into three types: Authentication Systems, Detection Systems, and Others. Authentication Systems ensure that only authorized individuals gain access, utilizing methods like key cards, biometric scans, or PIN codes. Detection Systems monitor and alert users about unauthorized access attempts or breaches, often employing video surveillance and intrusion alarms. The "Others" category encompasses various auxiliary technologies and solutions that enhance access control, such as smart locks or integrated building management systems.</p></p>
