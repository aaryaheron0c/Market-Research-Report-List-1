<p><ul><li>Linear Type</li><li>Cross-linked Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PPS resin market is divided into two primary types: linear and cross-linked. Linear PPS resin features a straight-chain structure, offering high flexibility and ease of processing, making it suitable for a variety of applications such as automotive and electrical components. In contrast, cross-linked PPS resin has a network structure that enhances thermal stability and mechanical strength, making it ideal for high-performance applications, including aerospace and industrial uses. Both types cater to specific demands in various industries.</p></p>
