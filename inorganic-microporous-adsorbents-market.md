<p><p>The inorganic microporous adsorbents market includes various types such as zeolites, clays, activated alumina, and silica gel. Zeolites are crystalline aluminosilicates used for gas separation and ion exchange. Clays serve as cost-effective adsorbents with good adsorption properties. Activated alumina, porous and highly efficient, is employed in drying and purification processes. Silica gel, widely recognized for its moisture-absorbing capabilities, is used in packaging to protect products from humidity. Each type serves distinct applications across industries like chemicals, pharmaceuticals, and environmental remediation.</p></p>
