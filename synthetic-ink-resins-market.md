<p><p>The synthetic ink resins market consists of various types that cater to diverse printing needs. Modified rosin resins enhance adhesion and stability, making them ideal for flexible packaging. Hydrocarbon resins offer excellent gloss and chemical resistance, suitable for high-performance inks. Acrylic resins provide durability and weather resistance, favored in outdoor applications.
