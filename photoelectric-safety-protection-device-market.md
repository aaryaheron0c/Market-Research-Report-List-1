<p><p>The Photoelectric Safety Protection Device market encompasses various output types, including PNP, NPN, and OSE outputs. PNP output devices use a positive voltage to activate the load, making them ideal for high-side switching applications. NPN output devices, on the other hand, ground the load, suitable for low-side switching. OSE (Opto-Sensor Output) devices utilize optical sensors for detecting external light levels, providing flexible integration with different control systems. Each type serves distinct requirements in safety and automation applications.</p></p>
