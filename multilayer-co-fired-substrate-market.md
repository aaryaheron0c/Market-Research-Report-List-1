<p><strong>The Multilayer Co-fired Substrate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LTCC Substrate</li><li>HTCC Substrate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The multilayer co-fired substrate market consists of two main types: LTCC (Low-Temperature Co-fired Ceramics) and HTCC (High-Temperature Co-fired Ceramics). LTCC substrates are utilized for applications requiring flexible design and integration of passive components at lower temperatures, making them suitable for RF and microwave circuits. In contrast, HTCC substrates, fired at higher temperatures, offer superior thermal stability and mechanical strength, ideal for high-frequency and high-power applications. Both types cater to diverse sectors, including telecommunications, automotive, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
