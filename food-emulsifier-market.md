<p><p>The food emulsifier market encompasses various types that enhance texture and stability in food products. Sorbitan esters act as stabilizers for creams and dressings, while polyglycerol esters provide emulsification in low-fat and gluten-free products. Together, these emulsifiers play a crucial role in food formulation, impacting quality and consumer experience.</p></p>
