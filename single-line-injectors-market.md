<p><p>Single-line injectors in the market are categorized into two main types: parallel and progressive. Parallel injectors operate under a uniform pressure across multiple lines, allowing for simultaneous fuel delivery to multiple cylinders, enhancing overall efficiency. In contrast, progressive injectors deliver fuel sequentially to each cylinder based on demand, optimizing performance and reducing wastage. Both systems aim to improve engine efficiency and emissions, but their operational mechanisms cater to different performance needs and efficiency requirements in various applications.</p></p>
