<p><strong>The Inline Water Heaters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Immersion Type</li><li>Indirect Immersion Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The inline water heater market comprises two main types: direct immersion and indirect immersion heaters. Direct immersion types involve heating elements immersed directly in the water, providing rapid heating and efficient energy use, ideal for applications with immediate hot water needs. In contrast, indirect immersion heaters use a surrounding heat exchange medium to warm the water, making them suitable for larger systems where water needs to be heated over time. Both types cater to various residential, commercial, and industrial applications.</p></p>
