<p><p>The HPLC (High-Performance Liquid Chromatography) instruments market comprises three main types: analyzers, sensors, and detectors. Analyzers are essential for separating and quantifying chemical compounds in samples. Sensors monitor various parameters, such as pressure and temperature, ensuring optimal performance and accuracy. Detectors, which include UV/Vis, fluorescence, and mass spectrometry, identify and quantify analytes as they elute from the column. Together, these components enhance the analytical capabilities of HPLC systems across various applications in pharmaceuticals, environmental testing, and food safety.</p></p>
