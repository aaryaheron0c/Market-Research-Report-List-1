<p><strong>The Fiber Optic MEMS Switches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-mode Switches</li><li>Multimode Switches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fiber Optic MEMS Switches market can be categorized into single-mode and multimode switches. Single-mode switches are designed for long-distance communication, providing low loss and high bandwidth by transmitting light through a single fiber core. In contrast, multimode switches are suited for shorter distances, allowing multiple light paths through a larger core, which enhances capacity but may increase modal dispersion. Both types are crucial in telecommunications, data centers, and other applications requiring efficient optical signal routing.</p></p>
