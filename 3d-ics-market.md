<p><strong>The 3D ICs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>3D SiCs</li><li>Monolithic 3D ICs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D IC market primarily consists of two types: 3D Silicon Chips (3D SiCs) and Monolithic 3D ICs. 3D SiCs involve stacking multiple silicon wafers or chips, enhancing performance and reducing latency through improved interconnectivity. In contrast, Monolithic 3D ICs are fabricated in a single process, integrating multiple functions on a single substrate, allowing for more compact designs and energy efficiency. Both technologies aim to address the increasing demand for higher performance in smaller form factors across various applications.</p></p>
