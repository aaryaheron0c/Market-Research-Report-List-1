<p><ul><li>Rf Microwave</li><li>Millimeter Wave</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Radio Frequency Transceiver Chip market is categorized into three main types: RF Microwave, Millimeter Wave, and Other. </p><p>RF Microwave transceivers operate in the frequency range of 1 GHz to 100 GHz, commonly used in communication and radar systems. Millimeter Wave transceivers function at higher frequencies, typically between 30 GHz and 300 GHz, enabling ultra-high-speed data transfer, essential for 5G applications. The "Other" category encompasses specialized transceivers used in niche applications, including industrial, medical, and satellite communications. Each type plays a crucial role in advancing wireless technologies.</p></p>
