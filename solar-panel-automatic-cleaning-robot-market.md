<p><p>The solar panel automatic cleaning robot market can be divided into two main types: trackless cleaning robots and railed cleaning robots. Trackless cleaning robots utilize advanced navigation technology to autonomously clean solar panels without the need for physical tracks, allowing for greater flexibility in movement and applicability to various installations. In contrast, railed cleaning robots operate along fixed tracks, ensuring precise cleaning over designated paths, which can enhance efficiency but may require more significant setup and infrastructure investment.</p></p>
