<p><p>The refractory gunning machine market is categorized into dry powder mixing and wet mixing types. Dry powder mixing machines utilize a dry mix of refractory materials, offering simplicity and ease of use for applications where moisture is undesirable. In contrast, wet mixing machines combine refractory materials with water or additives, enhancing material adhesion and reducing dust during application, making them suitable for more complex projects. Each type serves specific project needs based on material properties and application techniques.</p></p>
