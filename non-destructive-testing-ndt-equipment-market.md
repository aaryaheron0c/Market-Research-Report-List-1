<p><p>The Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) Equipment market encompasses various technologies to evaluate materials without causing damage. Eddy Current Testing uses electromagnetic induction to detect flaws in conductive materials. Magnetic Flux Leakage Testing identifies defects in ferromagnetic structures through magnetic field analysis. Ultrasonic Testing employs high-frequency sound waves to detect internal imperfections. Other methods include radiography, visual inspection, and ultrasonic thickness gauges. These techniques cater to diverse industries, ensuring structural integrity while maintaining the material's usability.</p></p>
