<p><p>Pricing guns are categorized into three types based on the number of lines they print: One Line, Two Line, and Three Line. One Line pricing guns print a single line of text, typically for basic price labeling. Two Line guns offer enhanced functionality by allowing a price and additional information, such as a barcode or product code. Three Line pricing guns are the most versatile, enabling the printing of a price, description, and other details, making them ideal for complex labeling needs in retail environments.</p></p>
