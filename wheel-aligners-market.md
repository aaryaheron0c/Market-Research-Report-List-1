<p><strong>The Wheel Aligners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Front end Alignment</li><li>Thrust-Angle Alignment</li><li>Four Wheel Alignment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wheel aligners market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Front-End Alignment** focuses on adjusting the front wheels to ensure they are parallel and meet vehicle specifications, enhancing steering stability and tire wear. </p><p>2. **Thrust-Angle Alignment** ensures that the rear wheels are aligned parallel to the front, optimizing tracking and stability during driving. </p><p>3. **Four-Wheel Alignment** involves comprehensive adjustments of all four wheels for comprehensive vehicle handling, improving overall performance and prolonging tire life. Each type addresses specific alignment needs for varied vehicle models.</p></p>
