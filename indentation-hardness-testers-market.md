<p><p>Indentation hardness testers are categorized based on their testing methods and applications. The Leeb hardness tester measures rebound hardness; the Brinell tester uses a spherical indenter for larger materials; the Rockwell tester applies different loads for rapid assessment; the Vickers tester employs a diamond indenter for versatility; the Knoop tester is ideal for brittle materials; the Shore tester measures elastomers and soft materials; and the Webster tester is designed for thin metals. Each type serves specific industrial needs.</p></p>
