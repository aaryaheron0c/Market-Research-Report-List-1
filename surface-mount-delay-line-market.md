<p><strong>The Surface Mount Delay Line Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive</li><li>Active</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Surface Mount Delay Line market is segmented into two primary types: passive and active delay lines. Passive delay lines function by using resistors, capacitors, and inductors to introduce a delay in signal transmission without the need for additional power. They are typically simpler and more cost-effective. In contrast, active delay lines utilize amplifying components like transistors and require power supply, offering more precise control over delay times and better performance in high-frequency applications. Both types serve distinct needs within electronic circuits.</p></p>
