<p><ul><li>Photoelectric</li><li>Ionization</li><li>Dual Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smoke alarm market comprises three primary types: photoelectric, ionization, and dual sensor detectors. Photoelectric alarms use a light beam to detect smoke particles and are effective for smoldering fires. Ionization detectors utilize radioactive material to sense fast-flaming fires through changes in ion flow. Dual sensor alarms combine both technologies, providing enhanced detection capabilities for various fire types. Each type caters to specific safety needs and preferences, influencing consumer choices and market dynamics.</p></p>
